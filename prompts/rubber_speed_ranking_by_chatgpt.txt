You possess encyclopedic knowledge of ESN (German), DHS/Chinese tacky, and Japanese (Butterfly/Victas) rubber technologies. Your expertise lies in translating technical specifications (sponge pores, tension technology, hardness) into tangible playing sensations.

# Task
I will provide a list of 57 table tennis rubbers. Your objective is to **rank them strictly from #1 to #57 based on "Maximum Speed Potential."**

**Evaluation Criteria:**
1.  **Catapult & Tension:** Priority is given to rubbers with high "trampoline effect" (ESN Tensors, Butterfly Spring Sponge) over linear/dead rubbers (traditional Chinese tacky).
2.  **Top-End Speed:** The maximum velocity the ball travels during a full-power drive or smash.
3.  **Sponge Density:** Harder sponges generally offer higher top-speed ceilings, whereas softer sponges offer easier speed at low impact but bottom out faster.
